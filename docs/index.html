Cribsheet


* Soccer matches modeled by Poisson distribution: goals_soccer ~ poisson(lambda = 1.4)

  • Average percentage of goalscoring shots on target taken that end up as goals: 31%.

  • Average percentage of goalscoring shots on target taken that end up as goals: 10.5% (Anything outside 7-12% will pretty much certainly not last - eventually)

  • Shots: At least 3.5 shots per 90 is typically good. Over 4 or 4.5 is pushing toward elite.

  • Previous research shows that a shot without defenders between the goal and ball goes in 11.59% on average vs 7.49% if defenders are present.

  • A fairly simple guideline for non-penalty scoring rates as a Forward:

    • .4 to .49 non-penalty goals per 90: Good.
    • .5 to .59 non-penalty goals per 90: Very good.
    • .6 to .79 non-penalty goals per 90: Probable Top 20 in Europe
    • .8 or above non-penalty goals per 90: Probable Top 5 in Europe
